Trainee Profiles:

It's very important to us at Hot Beans Web to hire people who share the same hopes and dreams as our founder does, people who have a clear passion and spark for website development, general coding or design. We want to give those people a space to grow their skills and expand their knowledge by working on new projects as a part of a team. One of the things that we are most proud of is our trainee team. Freshly graduated students, those from schools or other courses that fit what we do here, are welcome to join the many different training programs we have available. Trainees can expect high-quality lessons and interactive tutorials in which they can learn new things at the same time as gaining experience because our sessions will be focusing on real-life situations so they can gain the knowledge they will need in the future if they wish to develop their interests further. If you're unsure about whether you would be a good fit with us, take a look at some of our current trainees below, and who knows, you might just be surprised at how similar some of them might be to you.
